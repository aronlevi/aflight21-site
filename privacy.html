Privacy Policy

Aflight is operated under the Aflight21 brand. This page explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Aflight app or website.

Last updated: October 2025

Aflight21 (“we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Aflight mobile application or website.

Information We Collect

We collect the information you provide when creating an account or using features of the app, such as your name, email address, profile photo, and aviation-related posts. We may also collect analytics data and device information to improve performance.

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to provide, maintain, and improve the Aflight experience — including social features, mentoring, messaging, and app analytics.

We do not sell your personal information.

Third-Party Services

Aflight uses trusted third-party services, such as Firebase for authentication, database, and storage. These services may collect data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, including encryption and access controls. However, no online service is entirely risk-free, and you use the app at your own discretion.

Your Choices

You can request account deletion at any time through in-app settings, when available, or by emailing aflightapp@gmail.com.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at aflightapp@gmail.com.
